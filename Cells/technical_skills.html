NSS Technical Skills

Technology is just a tool. In terms of getting the kids working together and motivating them, the teacher is the most important.

Our Activity...

Spoken English

The Spoken English divison of NSS Technical Skill provides free Spoken English training to the village tem. Excelling them in the field of english grammar, pronounciation etc. They teach all the stuffs that would reduce the communciation gap among the students and the world.

Computer

The Computer divison of NSS Technical Skill provides free computer and technical education to the students of the village. We teach all those things that are required by an individual to fit himslef in this digital world. We have taught them to use all the basic softwares of computer. We have a future plan to teach them how to access the internet, ecommerce, e-banking, e-document chacking,. land papers checking and many other useful things.

Future Plans

Both the divisons work together under one umbrella "Technical Skills" with a motive to make the syudents self suffiecient to communicate with each other flawlessly and efficiently. Make them well acquainted with the digital world. Such that they become self sufficient to live a standard and helathy life. They should be able to make use of computer and mobile which has become an essential part of human life now a days.

What we Teach

Far far away, behind the word mountains, we have taught a lot. Few among them are...

Basic Knowledge

We have taught them all the basic key terms realted to computer and spoken english

MS Paint

We have taught them how to draw in paint. We have also made them familiar with the variuos features availabe in webpage.

WordPad

We have taught them about the various features of Wordpad and how to use it. We have made them practice via rigorous practice.

English Grammar

In Spoken English classe students are given through lessons of grammar. We have started teaching them every grammar they require for spoken English.

MS Word 2016

We have taught them about hte various features of MS Word and explained them why should we learn it. We have also conducted practical classes along with exams on this topic

English Pronounciation

Pronounciation matters a lot while speaking. Regular classes are conducted correcting the way in which the students speak.

Spoken English

Spoken English classes are conducted regularly expertising the student to speak fluently in English.

Notepad

We have first introduced text editor to the students by a simple editor Notepad.They have started learning to type in computer in this application.
