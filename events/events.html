Awareness Drive

On 6th January, 2019 the Technical Skill, Awareness, Rural Development cells together is going to organize an Awareness Campaign among the villagers of Amhara village. They will spread the need of Technical Education among the children's and also will provide ways to create self employment.

Plantation Drive

The plantation drive was conducted throughout the campus of IIT Patna by planting trees at various corner of the campus.

Cloth Collection & Donation

The volunteers of NSS conducted Cloth Collection Drive in Boys hostel, Girls hostel and faculty quaters. They have distributed those among the poor village people to give them warm in this freezy cold winter.
