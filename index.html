What we are?

The National Service Scheme of IIT Patna is an initiative to bring about a change in the Social and Economic forms of the Society.NSS believes that "Service to Mankind is Service to GOD" and with this mindset the members of NSS are ready to carve out humanity out of every social cause. The Scheme runs under Ministry of Sports and Youth Affairs, Govt of India with a motive to inculcate Social Responsibilty in the youth who will be tomorrow's work force of the Nation at Institute Level is headed by Dr. Rajgopal Rao, supported by the General Secretary Jagdish Anjana and Mentors of Different Cells who give guidance to nearly 120 Enthusiastic and Energetic Volunteers.


What we do..?

The NSS Society of IIT Patna functions in 6 various parts with its centre of activity being Amhara village.
It comprises of:


Rural Development----- Technical Skills----- Environmental
Teaching Group----- Prayatna----- Chetna Awareness Society
We conduct various sessions for teaching for JNVST and Technical skills Such as Paint,MS-Office,English to the students of Amhara Village at middle School Amhara.We make awareness among our college about various epidemic disease by continous publishing Posters and along with that at time of any Disaster we use to collect Funds for help.In the nearby Villages we strive to open new ways of earning by the technical knowledge of the volunteers and Work force of the People.
Prayatna is functioning on a large domain in many Nearby Village and established before any other Cell of NSS.


Our Motives..!

"Kindness but do not expect Gratitude"


We feel proud to contribute to a noble cause that hopes to change and enlighten the society and its people. It is delighting to see NSS growing day by day each day becoming more stonger. The members of NSS by their hard work and passion have started to make it a success. The future is yet to witness a change which we hope to bring in the society.










RECENT EVENTS

Cloth Collection and Donation

With an aim to improvise and giving a better life the Rural Development Team of NSS IIT Patna collected clothes that are donated by the Campus Staff and Faculty and Donated it to the Needy People of Nearby Area.

Kerala Flood Relief

The Chetna Team in collaboration with the Student's Gymkhana, along with other thousands of organisations across India, took up an initiative to collect funds for the unfortunate and needy people of Kerala. A handsome amount of funds were collected from the Boy's hostel, the Girl's hostel and the Faculty quarter of IIT Patna.


Our Cells

Technical Skills

Aiming towards increasing technical skills of the underprivileged children by providing them training in spoken English and Computer.

Rural Development

It mainly focuses on making potential people of nearby area entrepreneurs . This group cell tries for the implementation of various Projects in the direction of development of Village .

Environmental

• Management and Segregation of Waste into dry waste and wet waste inside our campus.
• Installation of a Biogas plant outside our campus under Unnat Bharat Abhiyan(UBA)
• Organise Plantation drives in our campus.

Chetna

We work to make people aware of their rights and different things that happen in society, especially to defend themselves against various malpractices that happen in society like Domestic violence, Child marriages, etc.
In other words, we are a push-up for the other teams of NSS.

Teaching

•Preparing the children of class 5th and 8th for Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Selection Test(JNVST).
• Making the children aware about various career opportunities.
•Empowering children not only intellectually but also socially for their all-round development.

Prayatana

This group of NSS plays a major role, it is an initiative to bring a change in the life of children who are the future of the country. it teaches them and provides them basic knowledge of topics of their respective syllabus.
