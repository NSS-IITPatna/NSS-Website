What we are..?

The national service scheme of IIT PATNA is an initiative to bring about a change in the social and economic forms of the society. NSS believes that "service to mankind is service to GOD " and with this mindset the members of nss are everready to carve out humanity out of every social cause. the sheme is headed by DR. Rajgopal Rao , supported by the general secretary Jagdish Anjana and its mentors.

What we do..?

The NSS Society of IIT Patna functions in 6 various parts with its centre of activity being Amhara village.
It comprises of:
Rural Development----- Technical Skills----- Chetna Awareness Society

Teaching Group----- Prayatna----- Environmental
We conduct various sessions for teaching for JNVST and Technical skills Such as Paint,MS-Office,English to the students of Amhara Village at middle School Amhara.We make awareness among our college about various epidemic disease by continous publishing Posters and along with that at time of any Disaster we use to collect Funds for help.In the nearby Villages we strive to open new ways of earning by the technical knowledge of the volunteers and Work force of the People.

RECENTS

CLOTH COLLECTION and DONATION

With an aim to improvise and giving a better life the Rural Development Team of NSS IIT Patna collected clothes that are donated by the Campus Staff and Faculty and Donated it to the Needy People of Nearby Area.

CLOTH COLLECTION

To provide the people of Anhara village an opportunity for earning a living we decided to produce cloth bags form waste cloth. As polythene is ban in BIHAR it was a good initiative. We collected the cloth form our campus and also set up some boxes so that people can frequently donate their waste clothes. Then we gave the material to ladies and we are trying our level best to prove them with maximum profit.

KERALA FLOOD RELIEF

The CHETNA team in collaboration with the student gymkhana, along with other thousands of organisations across India, took up an initiative to collect funds for the unfortunate and needy people of Kerala.A handsome amount of funds were collected from the boy's hostel, the girl's hostel and the faculty quarter of IIT Patna.

Our Cells

The NSS society of IIT Patna has six different committees whic have their own area of work.

Technical Skills

Aiming towards increasing technical skills of the underprivileged children by providing them training in spoken English and Computer.

Rural Development

• Aims to strengthen the Farmers who are the roots of soci ety and to uplift their social standards
• Focuses on creating employment and self helped groups for the unemployed

Environmental

• Management and Segregation of Waste into dry waste and wet waste inside our campus.
• Installation of a Biogas plant inside our campus.
• Organise Plantation drives in our campus.

Chetna

We work to make people awar e of their rights and different things that happen in society, especially to defend themselves against various malpractices that happen in society like D omestic violence, Child marriages, etc.
In other words, we are a push-up for the other teams of NSS.

Teaching

•Preparing the children of class 5th and 8th for Jawaharlal Nehru Vidyalaya Selection Test.
• Making the children aware about various career opportunities.
•Empowering children not only int ellectually but also socially for their all-round development.
