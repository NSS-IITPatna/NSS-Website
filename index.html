Our Cells

For smooth functioning of any organisation it must be divided in groups so to fulfill this motive our NSS team is divided into 5 groups.

Technical Skills

Formal education can't make a person complete to compete in today's world.Computer education along with english is also necessary.This stands as the aim for 'Technical Skills'.

Rural Development

Rural development is the emerging business in villages which takes place at low scale level.These include Agriculture,Fishery etc.Main motive of forming the group is to generate employment,income and reduce poverty in rural areas.

Environmental

It comprises of two sub groups Plantation and Cleanliness.It works to improve the health of environent both inside and outside the campus as well as organises various cleanliness drives paving path for swach bharat.

Awareness

It spreads information regarding various government schemes and organisations. It also organises various camps like blood donation camps.It also arranges various donations for a noble cause like Kerala Flood Donation.

Teaching

Its main aim is to prepare the village children of class 5th and 8th for Jwaharlal Nehru Vidyalya Selection Test.It also aware children about various carrer options.It also organises various interactive sessions with children and their parents.
