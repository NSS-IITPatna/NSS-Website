Our Motives..!

The main motive of our NSS team is to give back to society and instill the spirit of social service among youth.
Currently 120 students of NSS IIT Patna team divided in various groups work for upliftment of society through various means.

RECENTS

Technical Skills team imparted Computer Education to village children.

Technical skills team of IIT Patna visited Amhara village and give them basic knowledge about computers.
In future they are planning to give more advanced knowledge like msoffice,email etc.

Cloth Donation Drive

NSS IIT Patna team on 18-November-2018 donated old clothes to needy ones.It was made successful by the support of faculty and staff of IIT Patna , who donated around 200 clothes collected from faculty and staff quarters.
In future NSS team is planning to conduct more number of such events.
